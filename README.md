## Glossary
- **3-day Split**: A workout routine that divides training into three main days - typically Push (chest, shoulders, triceps), Pull (back, biceps), and Legs (lower body)
- **Aesop**: An Australian luxury skin, hair, and body care brand known for its plant-based formulations and minimalist aesthetic
- **Akis Petretzikis**: A popular Greek chef known for his YouTube cooking channel and easy-to-follow recipes
- **Amazon Prime**: A subscription service offering fast shipping, video streaming, and other benefits
- **Apple Calendar**: Apple's built-in calendar app that syncs across all Apple devices
- **Apple Fitness**: Apple's built-in fitness tracking app for iPhone and Apple Watch, offering workout tracking and activity monitoring
- **Apple Notes**: Apple's built-in note-taking app that syncs across all Apple devices
- **Backpack**: A web3 wallet primarily for the Solana blockchain ecosystem
- **Binance**: One of the world's largest cryptocurrency exchanges for trading digital assets
- **Bitcoin**: The first and most well-known cryptocurrency, a decentralized digital currency
- **Chase UK**: JPMorgan Chase's digital bank in the UK, offering savings accounts with competitive rates and cashback rewards
- **Coinbase**: A user-friendly cryptocurrency exchange and onramp platform
- **CoinGecko**: A cryptocurrency data aggregator providing prices, charts, and market information
- **Deliveroo**: A food delivery service platform
- **Discord**: A communication platform popular for community building and group chats
- **Google Calendar**: Google's web-based calendar service with cross-platform synchronization
- **Hims**: A telehealth company providing prescription and over-the-counter treatments for hair loss, skincare, and other men's health concerns
- **Hyperliquid**: A decentralized perpetuals trading platform
- **iMessage**: Apple's instant messaging service for iOS and macOS devices
- **Klarna**: A fintech company offering buy now, pay later services and flexible payment options
- **Kraken**: A cryptocurrency exchange known for its security and advanced trading features
- **La Roche Posay**: A French pharmaceutical skincare brand; their Effaclar Mat Moisturizer is specifically designed for oily skin
- **L-theanine**: An amino acid found in tea that promotes relaxation without drowsiness; often paired with caffeine for improved focus and reduced jitters
- **M&S**: Marks & Spencer, a British retailer known for quality food and clothing
- **MetaMask**: A cryptocurrency wallet and gateway to blockchain apps
- **NatWest**: A major British retail and commercial bank
- **Netflix**: A streaming service for movies, TV shows, and original content
- **PayPal**: An online payment system offering installment payment plans for purchases, allowing you to split payments over time
- **Perplexity.ai**: An AI-powered search and discovery platform that provides real-time news and information
- **Phantom**: A popular cryptocurrency wallet for the Solana blockchain
- **Raycast Notes**: A quick note-taking feature within Raycast, a productivity tool for macOS
- **Revolut**: A digital banking platform offering international money transfers, cryptocurrency trading, and multi-currency accounts
- **Slack**: A business communication platform for workplace messaging and collaboration
- **Third Space**: A luxury health club chain in London known for its high-end facilities and comprehensive fitness offerings
- **Trading212**: A financial platform offering commission-free stock trading and interest-bearing savings accounts
- **TradingView**: A platform for technical analysis and social network for traders and investors
- **Virgin Active**: A premium international health club chain offering various fitness facilities and classes
- **Waitrose**: A chain of British supermarkets
- **WhatsApp**: A popular messaging app for personal communication and group chats
- **Windsurf**: An advanced IDE (Integrated Development Environment) for coding
- **X.com**: Formerly known as Twitter, a social media platform for real-time news, discussions, and trends
- **Zoom**: A video conferencing platform for remote meetings and calls
